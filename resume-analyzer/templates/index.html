Resume Tips

  • Include your full contact information and professional summary
  • List technical skills with specific versions (e.g., "Python 3.9", "React 18")
  • Use action verbs and quantify achievements (e.g., "Improved performance by 30%")
  • Include relevant projects with technologies used
  • Match keywords from job description naturally in your content

Job Description Tips

  • Copy the complete job posting including requirements section
  • Include both "Required" and "Preferred" qualifications
  • Don't edit or summarize - paste the full original text
  • Include company culture and role responsibilities for better matching
  • Make sure technical requirements are clearly listed

  • Provides specific recommendations for improvement
  • Highlights missing keywords that could boost your chances
